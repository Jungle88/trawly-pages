## Who We Are

**Enviz** converts 3D architectural models into immersive AR/VR experiences for real estate. Property developers and agencies use us to sell off-the-plan properties before they're built — buyers can walk through a home that doesn't exist yet.

**What we do:** A developer gives us their 3D model → we transform it into an interactive AR/VR experience → their sales team uses it to sell properties faster. We're the bridge between architectural design and buyer experience.

**Why it matters:** Off-the-plan property is a massive market. Buyers struggle to visualise a home from floor plans and renders. We solve that. Our experiences increase buyer confidence, accelerate pre-sales, and give developers a competitive edge.

**Where we're at:** We're a growing team of ~15 people — sales, marketing, engineering, customer success. We have product-market fit and paying customers. The priority now is scaling revenue and making the team more efficient. That's where you come in.
