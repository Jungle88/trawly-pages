"The Problem Wasn't the AI. It Required the User to Do Work."
Day 10 of building in public: A founder shares the lesson that killed a 3-month AI feature. Users tried it twice and stopped. Not because of the AI — but because it required them to provide context, read output, and act on it. Martha's Rule Has "Saved Hundreds of Lives" in England — Named After a 13-Year-Old Who Died of Sepsis

Martha's rule launched in September 2024. Since then, more than 10,000 calls have been made to the helpline.
