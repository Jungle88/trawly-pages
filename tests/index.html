💡 Key insight: At scale, the difference between A/B/C becomes negligible — they converge to ~$0.35/user/day (compile cost). The X API and research cost becomes a flat overhead shared across all users. The only cost that scales linearly is the per-user compile step. Choose the methodology that produces the best quality, not the cheapest one — the cost difference at scale is pennies per user.
