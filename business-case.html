5. Gross Margin Analysis

At $19/mo pricing: $19 - $12 COGS = $7 profit per user = 63% gross margin at 20 users.

At scale (1,000+ users with cohort optimisation): $19 - $6.33 = $12.67 profit per user = 67% gross margin. At 10K users: ~$19 - $3.03 = 84% gross margin.

Comparison: Morning Brew ~85%, The Information ~70%, Trawly 63-84% (improving with scale)
