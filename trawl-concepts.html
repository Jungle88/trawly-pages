Concept A — The Net Grid
TRAWLY
THE NET IS THE LOGO
The dot grid = trawl net. Bright nodes = gems caught. Fades from surface light at edges to abyss black at center. Every intersection is a potential discovery point. The grid pattern works at any scale — favicon to billboard.
Concept B — The Deep Cast
TRAWLY
THE T IS THE TRAWL BOOM
The horizontal bar of the T = the trawl boom/outrigger. Lines descend like cables into the deep. The deeper you go, the darker it gets — and that's where the gems glow. Depth markers on the side like a sonar reading. The T literally casts the net.
Concept C — The Catenary
TRAWLY
GEMS IN THE BELLY OF THE NET
The catenary curve — how a net actually hangs under its own weight — creates an elegant mathematical form. Gems collect at the deepest point. The waterline divides surface noise from deep value. Bioluminescent particles hint at more treasures below.
